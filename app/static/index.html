AI providers

        

          Two slots: the OpenAI-shape slot powers transaction-suggest, bank-statement parsing, and other classic AI features. The Anthropic-shape slot powers the conversational AI Chat (tool use, action cards, undo). You can use one Metis account for both by pointing each slot at the matching Metis URL (/openai/v1 and /anthropic/v1).

Reporting locale

        

          Choose the financial statement template. Iranian standard matches رهنمودهای استاندارد حسابداری ایران (TSE-listed format). UK uses the FRS 102 Section 1A format from Companies Act 2006.
        

        

          

            
            
          

          

            
          

          

        


        

          

            
            
          

          

            
          

          

        

        

          Dates are always stored as Gregorian internally. The calendar
          setting affects how report headers and transaction-list dates are
          displayed. HTML date inputs remain Gregorian.
