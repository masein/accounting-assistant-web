What's inside

Everything a small business needs to keep clean books

A full accounting back office — not just a chatbot. The AI is one way in; everything is also available through the dashboard, reports, and APIs.

Conversational bookkeeping

Record transactions, look up balances and answer questions in natural language — in English, Persian, Spanish or Arabic.

The AI can't touch the ledger

Every write goes through propose → confirm → execute, with idempotency tokens, a 30-second undo window and a full audit trail.

Double-entry, done right

Balanced vouchers, a hierarchical chart of accounts, trial balance, and a self-audit that flags duplicate payments and anomalies.

Multi-locale statements

Iranian standard statements (صورت سود و زیان) and UK FRS 102 Section 1A — with prior-year comparatives that reconcile.

Jalali calendar & multi-currency

Native Shamsi (Jalali) dates, Toman/Rial/GBP and FX handling — figures are always labelled in your reporting currency.

Invoicing, OCR & reconciliation

Sales/purchase invoices, receipt scanning, bank-statement import and reconciliation, recurring rules, budgets and inventory.

CFO & owner dashboard

KPIs, a 13-week cash-flow forecast, AR/AP aging, profitability by client and a data-health score at a glance.

Native Apple apps

Companion iOS, macOS and watchOS clients with voice input talk to the same API — log expenses from your wrist.

Provider-neutral AI

Bring your own model: Anthropic, any OpenAI-compatible endpoint, or a fully local LM Studio model. No lock-in.

Safe by design

How a chat becomes a journal entry

The model proposes; you decide. Nothing hits the books without an explicit, auditable confirmation.

1

You ask

"Pay the £900 telecom bill from the BT invoice." The assistant parses amount, date, entity and accounts.

2

It proposes

A balanced draft entry is written to a pending table — never to the ledger — and returned with a confirmation token.

3

You confirm

One click executes it idempotently, posts the journal entry and writes a single audit-log row tagged to the AI.

4

Undo anytime

A 30-second window reverses the entry with a paired audit record — so mistakes never become permanent.
